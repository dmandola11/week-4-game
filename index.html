Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specified amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to a random number and lose the game if your score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts the value of each crystal will change. Good luck!
